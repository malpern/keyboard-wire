Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2026

What we collect

If you subscribe to our newsletter via Buttondown, we store your email address for the sole purpose of sending you keyboard news digests. We do not sell, share, or rent your email to anyone.

Cookies and tracking

This site is static HTML hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not use cookies, analytics, tracking pixels, or any third-party scripts. There is nothing to opt out of because there is nothing to opt into.

Third-party services

  • GitHub Pages hosts this site. GitHub may log IP addresses per their own privacy policy.
  • Buttondown manages newsletter subscriptions. Their privacy policy is at buttondown.com/legal/privacy.
  • X (Twitter) receives automated posts of news items. Interacting with those posts is governed by X's privacy policy.

Content sources

Items on this site are curated from public sources: Hacker News, Reddit, and vendor newsletters. Email content is sanitized to remove personal information before publication.

Your rights

You can unsubscribe from the newsletter at any time via the link in any email. To request deletion of your data, email malpern@gmail.com.
