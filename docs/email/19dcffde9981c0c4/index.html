Mode Designs announces 2026 Encore with Crown Mounting System

A year of refinement, the new Crown Mounting System, and two films to show the work.

The Crown Mounting System is the heart of the new Encore.

Our two new videos go deeper than a spec sheet. The first walks through the 2026 colorways and how each finish was developed.

The second takes you inside the Crown Mounting System itself—from the engineering decisions, the iterations, and the result. You can also read about the development on our new blog.

Pre-orders for the 2026 Encore featuring our brand new Crown Mounting System are available now. Fulfillment is estimated to begin this July.
